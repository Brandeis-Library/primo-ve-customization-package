Get Help with OneSearch and More

We have a variety of resources to help you with your research. Here is a short list to help you get started.

  • To learn how to make the most of OneSearch, visit this guide
  • To learn more about our most unique and special collections-most of which you can discovery right here in OneSearch-please visit the Robert D. Farber University Archives & Special Collections website
  • To learn more about your borrowing privileges, please visit the library's borrowing website
  • To learn more about obtaining resources from other libraries or our scan on demand service, please visit the Interlibrary Loan and Scan on Demand website
  • To get help with your research topic, use the slider to the right of your screen that says "Ask a Librarian" or visit the library website for more options
  • To get help with campus technologies outside the library, please consult the ITS website
